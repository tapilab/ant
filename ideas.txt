Just use a normal PSQL backed Django ORM with classes:

EntityType:
  - name : str

Entity:
  - EntityType
  - Value[]

Field:
  - EntityType
  - field_name : str

Value:
  - Field
  - EntityType
  - value : str

RelationType
  - EntityType left
  - EntityType right
  - label : str
  - symmetric : bool

Relation:
  - RelationType
  - Entity : left
  - Entity : right


  (artist, name, 'caravaggio')
  (creator_artist_artwork, 'caravaggio', 'st john the baptist')


  (artist, name)
  (artist, birth_year)

  (creator, artist, artwork, symmetric=False): key=(creator, artist, artwork)
